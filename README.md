# Koyalpara-premier-league-

It's a football auction game 

## 🏆 PvP Auction League

A complete fantasy football auction game where managers draft players, bid on elite talent, and compete in a tournament league.

## Features

- **Manager Registration**: Setup up to 8 managers for your league
- **Club Selection**: Each manager picks a real football club
- **Coach Selection**: Choose tactical managers with unique abilities
- **Live Auction System**: Bid on 350+ fantasy players including football legends
- **Squad Building**: Build 11-player squads with $500M budget
- **League Play**: Compete in round-robin tournament
- **Final Standings**: View complete league table with W/D/L records

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Register all managers (2-8 players)
3. Select your club and coach
4. Participate in the live auction - bid on players for your squad
5. Select your team formation
6. Watch the tournament unfold and see final standings
